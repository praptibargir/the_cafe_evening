About Us

Established in 2018 by Akash Agrawal, Cafe Evening is a cozy retreat nestled in the heart of Biaf Road, Wagholi. We pride ourselves on offering a warm and inviting atmosphere where friends, families, and colleagues can gather to unwind and indulge in delectable treats.

At Cafe Evening, we're passionate about serving quality coffee, mouthwatering snacks, and delightful desserts. Our menu features a diverse selection of beverages, including specialty coffees, teas, refreshing juices, etc. Whether you're craving a cheese burger, a loaded pizza, or a refreshing iced tea, we have something to satisfy every plate.
